# Code of Conduct

We, as members, contributors, and maintainers of the AI Moderated Encyclopedia project, pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- **Be Respectful:** Treat everyone with respect. Engage in constructive discussions and refrain from personal attacks.
- **Be Considerate:** Remember that your contributions help shape a welcoming community. Listen to feedback and be open to constructive criticism.
- **Be Collaborative:** Share your ideas, support fellow contributors, and work together to build a positive environment.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include, but are not limited to:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination based on any personal characteristic.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Posting or sharing content that is obscene, offensive, or inappropriate.
- Any behavior that could be reasonably considered harmful or disruptive to the community.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior. This may include warning the offender, temporarily or permanently banning them from the project, or removing content that violates this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by contacting the project maintainers at [insert contact information or instructions]. All reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.
